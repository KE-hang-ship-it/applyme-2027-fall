## Key Findings

1. **All 38 schools are displaying National University rankings instead of Mechanical Engineering rankings** - This is the primary data integrity issue.

2. **Georgia Tech (currently #33) should display Mechanical Engineering #5** - Most significant ranking discrepancy.

3. **UC Berkeley (currently #15) should display Mechanical Engineering #3** - Major underrepresentation.

4. **Purdue (currently #46) should display Mechanical Engineering #7** - Massive underrepresentation.

5. **Virginia Tech (currently #51) should display Mechanical Engineering #17** - Outside Top 50 in National, but Top 20 in ME.

6. **17 Top 50 Mechanical Engineering programs are missing** - Including Caltech, Harvard, Yale, and Penn State.

7. **3 schools in the library are outside ME Top 50** - NYU, Tufts, Rochester.

---

## Recommendations

1. **Immediate**: Fix ranking display logic to show Mechanical Engineering rankings for ME programs.

2. **High Priority**: Add Caltech, Harvard, Yale, Penn State, Texas A&M to the library.

3. **Medium Priority**: Add remaining 12 missing schools from ME Top 50.

4. **Data Quality**: Create separate fields for `nationalUniversityRanking` and `mechanicalEngineeringRanking` in the Program type.
